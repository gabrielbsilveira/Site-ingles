About The Series


"Money, Explained" is a series that delves into the financial universe, exploring everything from basic concepts of economics and personal finance to pitfalls like credit cards, casinos, scams, and student loans.

Each episode offers valuable insights and strategies to navigate financial obstacles, presented engagingly by experts.

Review


"Money Explained" is a Netflix documentary series that talks about money in a simple and easy-to-understand way. The series uses animations and real-life examples to explain different aspects of money, such as how we spend, save, and lose it.

The series does a great job of breaking down complicated financial concepts into basic ideas that anyone can understand. It covers important topics like credit cards, scams, gambling, retirement, and the idea of getting rich quickly. Each topic is presented in a clear and engaging way, making it easy to follow and learn.

Review


One of the strengths of "Money Explained" is how it makes finance interesting and accessible. The animations and examples help to keep the viewer's attention and make the information easier to remember. The series also gives practical tips on how to manage money better and avoid common financial mistakes.

However, the series can sometimes feel a bit repetitive, and some topics might need more detail. Even so, it provides a good overview of important financial issues and is a great starting point for anyone who wants to understand money better.

In summary, "Money Explained" is an educational and entertaining series that helps viewers learn about money in a simple and clear way. It is perfect for beginners who want to get a better grasp of their finances.
